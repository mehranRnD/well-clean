Commercial Cleaning

Our commercial cleaning services include:

  • Offices & Corporate Spaces: Regular cleaning of workstations, meeting rooms, kitchens, and reception areas.
  • Education & Leisure Facilities: Classrooms, restrooms, gyms, libraries, sports halls, and event venues.
  • Healthcare Facilities: Patient rooms, examination areas, surgical theatres, waiting lounges, and sanitary stations, all cleaned to NHS hygiene standards.
  • Hospitality & Retail: Shops, cafés, restaurants, hotel lobbies, and communal areas, with high-touch surface disinfection.

  • Ad-hoc Services: Emergency cleans, post-event cleanups, or one-off deep cleans as required.

With flexible scheduling and industry-specific expertise, Well Clean Services Ltd ensures your business looks its best—day in, day out.

Domestic Cleaning

Our domestic cleaning services include:

  • General Home Cleaning: Living rooms, kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and hallways, with vacuuming, dusting, and sanitising throughout.
  • Deep Cleans: Top-to-bottom attention, including behind appliances, inside cupboards, and descaling taps and tiles.
  • End-of-Tenancy Cleans: Move-out cleaning to meet landlord or agency standards, ensuring a stress-free handover.
  • After-Party & Occasion Cleans: Perfect for post-event recovery, returning your home to a tidy and fresh state.
  • Holiday Let & Airbnb Cleaning: Turnaround cleans with linen change and restocking where needed.
  • Eco-Friendly Cleans: Safe and sustainable products for families, pets, and allergy-sensitive homes.

With flexible appointment times and personalised cleaning plans, Well Clean Services Ltd takes care of your home—so you can focus on what matters most.
